NewRecord

this function insert new record to your recordset and empty all related controls within your form and its grid

Syntax:

Function NewRecord(ByRef dm_Form As System.Windows.Forms.Form, ByRef dm_MasterTable As ADODB.Recordset, Optional ByRef dm_Grid As AxMSDataGridLib.AxDataGrid = Nothing, Optional ByRef dm_DetailTable As ADODB.Recordset = Nothing)

Example:

Dim oMaster As New ADODB.Recordset()
Dim oDetails As New ADODB.Recordset()

DM.NewRecord(Me, oMaster, MyGrid, oDetails)

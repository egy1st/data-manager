GoPrevious

this function move your recordset back one record and populate your form and its grid with related fields

Syntax:

Function GoPrevious(ByRef dm_Form As System.Windows.Forms.Form, ByRef dm_MasterTable As ADODB.Recordset, Optional ByRef dm_Grid As AxMSDataGridLib.AxDataGrid = Nothing, Optional ByRef dm_DetailTable As ADODB.Recordset = Nothing)

Example:

Dim oMaster As New ADODB.Recordset()
Dim oDetails As New ADODB.Recordset()

DM.GoPrevious(Me, oMaster, MyGrid, oDetails)

CloseForm

this function close your form and all related recordset

Syntax:

Function CloseForm(ByRef dm_Form As System.Windows.Forms.Form, ByRef dm_MasterTable As ADODB.Recordset, Optional ByRef dm_DetailTable As ADODB.Recordset = Nothing)

Example:

Dim oMaster As New ADODB.Recordset()
Dim oDetails As New ADODB.Recordset()

CloseForm(Me oMaster, oDetails)

Search

Easily to search your recordset , add button in your form and notify Manipulation Buttons Function with its name

  1. Begin search by clicking search button , all form controls will be empty , now you get in Search Mode

  2. Enter your pattern to search and click search button again , now your form has the search result

  3. Navigate through records which apply your search pattern by Navigation Buttons

  4. Return to Normal Mode by click search button twice
